
This is a checklist to follow when adding a utility:

*) .gitignore

*) Makefile.am: bin_PROGRAMS and foo_LDADD

*) remove from doc/status/UNIMPLEMENTED

*) create doc/status/foo.status (and add to doc/status/Makefile.am)

*) if it has (or will have) translations, add to po/POTFILES.in

*) add man page (and add to doc/man/Makefile.am)

*) add at least a simple "it works" test to tests/ and modify
   doc/tests/Makefile.am accordingly

*) make distcheck

*) make update-po

*) mention in NEWS document
